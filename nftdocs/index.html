Introduction

Welcome to the documentation for the NFT Generator. This guide explains the technical concepts behind generative art and provides a live demonstration of layer stacking.

Note: The traits shown here are samples only. You can request custom traits and base NFTs for your own collection.

How It Works

The generator works by stacking transparent PNG images (layers) in a specific order using CSS z-index.

Layer Order (Top to Bottom)

  1. Headwear (z-index: 6)
  2. Eyes (z-index: 5)
  3. Beak (z-index: 4)
  4. Outfit (z-index: 3)
  5. Body (z-index: 2)
  6. Background (z-index: 1)

Rarity Calculation

The total supply is determined by the Cartesian product of all layer options:

Total = Count(Background) × Count(Body) × Count(Outfit) × Count(Beak) × Count(Eyes) × Count(Headwear)
